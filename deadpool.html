Deadpool is the alter ego of Wade Wilson, a Canadian mercenary with superhuman healing abilities. He is known for his tendency to joke incessantly and break the fourth wall for humorous effect.










See 5 facts about the movie


  1. Ryan Reynolds: Ryan Reynolds plays Deadpool, who has the same physical characteristics as Reynolds.


  2. Name: In the comics, Wade Wilson chose the name Deadpool while being experimented on by Ajax and Dr. Killebrew. The duo ran a betting pool called a "dead pool".


  3. Insanity: In the Marvel comic Deadpool Kills The Marvel Universe, Deadpool becomes insane and murders the entire cast of Marvel characters.


  4. Immortality: T-Ray, an agent of Thanos, resurrects Deadpool and curses him with immortality so he can never see Death again.


  5. Captain America: Deadpool's idol is Captain America.
